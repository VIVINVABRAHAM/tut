How do I place an ad?

Sign Up yourself to Tit-Tat,An email will get to your email which you have given.Based on that you can Sign in. User can add advertisement from the Post Ads button.There user can select the category of the product based on that he can add the details of the product. And the user should update 4 images of the product(if he didn't have 4 image, upload the Same image repeatedly). Then add the demanded product

Demanded product is always user's wish product which we like to get return back. Most of the time we fail to get that.So kindly add the product which has more prior to you.You will get notified someone is interested to your product. If you satisfy with that product then you can accept it.
